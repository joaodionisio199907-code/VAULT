About the game

In this experience that combines horror, comedy, and puzzles, you will play as a demon who has been summoned by an old woman to fix her television. This seemingly simple task will embark you on an adventure beyond the limits of reason, exploring impossible spaces and overcoming twisted obstacles. Main features: • Exclusive mechanics. Change the environment using your gaze; revealing clues, turning on lights, activating hidden passages… · An atmosphere that blends horror and comedy , driving forward a story told from the heart. • Chase segments. Face off against creatures from beyond that will get your heart racing. • Immersive audio design. Sound plays a crucial role in the horror experience, generating its own sonic language that amplifies the tension. • Original soundtrack. The music will accompany you throughout the journey, enveloping you in moments of terror and lifting you up in the most epic challenges. This game is ideal for fans of horror and puzzle experiences. It has been designed with great attention to detail to deliver a focused and impactful experience in a short amount of time. Every element of the game has been carefully crafted to provide an unforgettable journey.
